Welcome to Los Carrochos,
a sleepy little Mexican village. Old men nap in front of buildings, children play among the cactuses, buxom Mexican lasses gossip around the town fountain. Sounds rather picturesque, no?

Well, it might be, if this were any ordinary sleepy little Mexican village.

Unfortunately, this village is the hometown and haunt of...
EL BEANO!
