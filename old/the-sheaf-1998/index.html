That's right, the Sheaf Turns 85 this year!
We're always looking for volunteers, so the best way to get involved is to come down and introduce yourself. Our office is Room 108 MUC (just above Louis'). Another way to get involved is to attend our Collective meetings which are held every Thusday at 2:30 pm in the Sheaf office.
Anyone in the university community can contribute to the Sheaf. If writing is not your thing, we also need proofers, photographers, cartoonists, webpage designers, and delivery help. After any three contributions to the paper, you will be voted onto the Sheaf Collective. This entitles you to vote on issues concerning the paper, as well as the opportunity to join the many Sheaf events throughout the year.
Naturally, the Sheaf is interested in serving its readership, this means you. If you have any story ideas, feed back, or suggestions that could contribute to our paper, we would like to hear from you.
